Our Story

Transforming Ethiopia's Internship Landscape

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between education and industry, WBIPS has become Ethiopia's leading internship placement platform.

2020 - Foundation

WBIPS was founded to address the internship placement challenges in Ethiopian universities.

2021 - First Partnerships

Partnered with 5 universities and 25 companies for our pilot program.

2022 - Platform Launch

Officially launched the digital platform with 500+ students and 50+ companies.

2023 - Expansion

Expanded to 15+ universities with 2,500+ students and 150+ partner companies.
